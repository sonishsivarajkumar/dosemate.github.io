Our Story

Founded in 2024, DoseMate emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: making medication management accessible to everyone, regardless of language barriers. Our team of healthcare professionals and technology experts came together to create an innovative solution that combines advanced AI with user-friendly design.

Mission

To eliminate language barriers in healthcare and empower everyone to manage their medications safely and effectively.

Vision

To become the global standard for multilingual medication management, making healthcare more accessible worldwide.
